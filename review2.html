Review: In Rainbows by Radiohead

Artist: Radiohead

Album: In Rainbows

Rating: 4.8 / 5

Date Reviewed: September 2, 2024

"In Rainbows" is a beautifully crafted album that blends the experimental sounds of Radiohead with a warm, inviting tone. Tracks like "Weird Fishes/Arpeggi" and "Nude" highlight the band's ability to create immersive soundscapes while still retaining emotional depth.
